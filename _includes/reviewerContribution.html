Support us as a review team member

 



Just as in previous years, we want the Agile Tour Vienna 2018 to feature a high-quality and well-balanced schedule. You can help us actively shape this year’s schedule by becoming a review team member.

How can you get on board?
  • Send your curriculum vitae to info@agiletourvienna.at by May 31, 2018
  • Include a cover letter describing why you are the perfect fit for our review team
What do we need from you?
  • Attend three meetings (in September and October) at the TechTalk Office in Vienna
  • Motivate and encourage people in your network to submit proposals
  • Read and evaluate every proposal (approx. 50)
  • Very good German and English skills; proposals can be submitted in both German and English
