  1. June 1st, 2017

    Call for Proposals Starts

    We invite all agile practitioners to present their talks at Agile Tour 2017. The main conference language is German, but talks in English are also welcome.

    Topics

    Talks or workshops should cover topics from one of the following areas:

    Organization in agile projects or companies
    Technical best practices and useful tools
    Requirements and testing in agile projects
    Economic questions, project performance and business considerations

    In particular, we are looking for contributions involving customers and real life case studies (the more state-of-the-art and experimental, the merrier â€“ people who dare to try should be on stage). Please bring your customer or customer project with you.

    Session Formats

    Possible session formats (each 45 minutes) are:

    Classical talk
    Interactive sessions / workshops
    Practitioner report (case studies)

    If your proposed session involves longer exercises or a special format, you can also offer a session requiring two 45-minute slots.

    You will also have the opportunity to engage in discussion with other attendees in an Open Jam area, where you can post topics and questions that anyone can read and reply to.

  2. October 2nd, 2017

    Applicants Receive Feedback

    During this time, all applicants will have access to the current state of their reviews and can improve their proposal and submit a response. Reviews are submitted by the content committee and reviewers from the community.

    Responding to Feedback

    Please keep the following in mind during this process:

    Your response should focus on any questions or misunderstandings raised by the reviewers.
    The review response period is an opportunity to react to the reviews, but not a requirement to do so. If you feel the reviews are accurate and the reviewers have not asked any questions, you do not have to respond.
    Reviews are as submitted by the PC members without coordination between them. This may lead to inconsistencies.
    The program committee will read your responses carefully and take this information into account during the final decision.

    To submit your response you should log on and select your submission from the menu. If you need help, refer to these step-by-step instructions.

  3. Applicants Submit Response to Feedback

    October 16th, 2017

    This is the deadline for all applicants to submit their responses, which will hopefully resolve any outstanding questions and issues. Based on the reviews, the Content Committee will start to create the schedule and select the final speakers. You will be of their decision, whether you are accepted or not.

  4. October 31st, 2017

    Final Speaker Announcement

    The schedule is finalized and will be announced on the website.
