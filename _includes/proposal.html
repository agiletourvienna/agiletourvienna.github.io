Call for Proposals

We invite all agile practitioners to join Agile Tour 2016 with their talk.
The main conference language is German but English talks are appreciated too.

Topics

Talks or Workshops should cover topics out of one of the following areas:

  • Organizational topics in agile projects or companies
  • Technical best practices and wonderful tools
  • Requirements and Testing in agile projects

Economic questions, project performance and business considerations In particular we’re looking for contributions that involve customers and real life case studies (the more state-of-the-art and experimental, the merrier – people who dare to try should be on stage). Please bring your customer or customer project with you.

Session Formats

Possible session formats (each 45 minutes) are:

  • Classical talk
  • Interactive sessions / workshops
  • Practitioner report (case studies)

For proposals with longer exercises or special formats you can offer sessions that require two 45-minute slots.

We’ll also provide an opportunity to discuss with other attendees in an Open Jam area where you can post topics or questions for all to see and participate.

Deadline and Required Information

Please download and fill in the “Speaker Proposal” template to prepare your submission (CallForProposalsForm2016.docx). You can also download a previously accepted proposal as example on how to write a suitable proposal. There is one available in English and one in German.

  • Proposal submission deadline – September 9th, 2016
  • Acceptance notification – September 23rd, 2016
  • Confirmation to participate (of you) -  September 29th, 2016

Send your complete proposal to info@agiletourvienna.at by September 9th latest. The program committee will confirm your talk at latest by September 23rd, but we’ll also start accepting individual talks earlier.
