It's about agile

The Agile Tour Vienna is a non-profit event that aims at bringing together experts, practioners and everybody else who is interested in agile software development.

International Speakers

We are trying to get the best experts, in the field of agile development, to Vienna every year. In addition we want to accomplish the perfect tradeoff between ticket prices and international known speakers.

Global

The main conference language is German but we welcome talks in English and are looking forward to an exchange with international participants.
